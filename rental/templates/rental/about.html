About

Car rental

There is a list of Cars in the system for which it is necessary to implement:

  • selection by brand;
  • selection by quality class;
  • sorting by name.

The client does registration in the system, selects a car and makes a rental order. An unregistered customer cannot place an order.

In the order data, the client indicates the passport data, the option 'with driver' / 'without driver', rental period.

The system generates a bill that the customer pays.

The manager reviews the order and may reject it, indicating the reason.

The manager also records the return of the car, in case of damage being done to the car, bills through the system for repair.

The system administrator owns the rights:

  • adding, deleting cars, editing information about cars;
  • blocking / unblocking users;
  • registration of managers in the system.
